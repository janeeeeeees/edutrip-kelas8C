About Stitch

Stitch (also known as Experiment 626) is the fictional protagonist of Disney's Lilo & Stitch franchise. Created by Chris Sanders, this genetically engineered, blue koala-like alien creature possesses great strength and intelligence, along with a mischievous streak.

"Ohana means family. Family means nobody gets left behind or forgotten."

Originally designed to cause chaos across the galaxy, Stitch escapes and crash-lands on Earth where he meets Lilo, a lonely Hawaiian girl who adopts him as her "dog." Through their friendship, Stitch learns about love, family, and the true meaning of "ohana."
